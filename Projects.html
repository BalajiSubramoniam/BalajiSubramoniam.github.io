Past:

  1. Examples of Infinity Categories (Master's Dissertation)
    2. I wrote my Master's dissertation under the guidance of Prof. Neil Strickland and Prof. Sukhendu Mehrotra during my final year at CMI. Roughly, the aim of the dissertation is to compare locally 0-truncated infinity categories and ordinary categories. The comparison statement is a low-hanging fruit of definitions and tools introduced earlier, which are to some extent, technical. Send me an email if you would like a copy of the draft. In my opinion, you might find the draft useful only in the following cases:
    1. You are interested in the rudiments of the theory of infinity categories (more precisely, quasicategories). However, I must confess that there are many (in my honest opinion) better sources (See for example Groth's or Rezk's notes) that serve this purpose.
    2. You are interested in locally truncated ∞-categories.
    3. You are interested in certain definitions of "(∞,2)-Categories" (Weak ∞-bicategories, ∞-bicategories and (∞,2)-categories) and how they compare. This is done in these papers of Lurie and Gagna, Harpaz and Lanari . The dissertation elaborates on some aspects of this along with a few supporting proofs that supplement the literature.
