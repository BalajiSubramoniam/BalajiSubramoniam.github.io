AIS - Cohomology of Commutative Algebras (Lecture Notes)

This page contains a compilation of lecture notes from an Advanced Instructional School titled ``Cohomology of Commutative Algebras'' organised at IIT Madras from 02 December 2024 to 14 December 2024 by Prof. Manoj Kummini and Prof. Sarang Sane. The workshop consisted of six lecture series, delivered by Prof. Manoj Kummini, Prof. Sukhendu Mehrotra, Prof. Anjan Gupta, Prof. Srikanth Iyengar, Prof. Benjamin Briggs and Prof. Anand Sawant. More information on the workshop can be found here.

These notes have significantly benefited from all the lectures, discussions with speakers, organisers, tutors and fellow participants, and from the lecture accounts of participants Abhiram Subramanian and Siddhi Ambhore. Proofreading, typesetting and maintenance of this page are due to Balaji Subramoniam, who is also solely responsible for all errors here. Comments and corrections are most welcome and may be emailed to balajisubramoniam@gmail.com.

[Update (14.03.25): This page supersedes an older one at the same link and will be updated regularly (starting today) -- expectedly at the rate of four lectures a week. Occasionally, these notes might contain some supplementary remarks for personal reference. In order to distinguish such remarks from the actual contents of the lecture, these always appear in brown text.
